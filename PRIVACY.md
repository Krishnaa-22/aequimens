# Aequimens Privacy Summary

_Last updated: 19 July 2026_

Aequimens is designed to work without an account. Wellness information is stored locally in the browser on the device where the app is used.

## Information stored locally

Aequimens may store:

- Profile preferences such as preferred name, age, and routine times
- Check-in answers and wellness scores
- Missions, habits, routines, goals, and completion history
- Journal entries and context markers
- Reminder preferences and privacy-lock settings

## Data use

The app uses this information only to provide local features such as daily plans, charts, search, weekly reflections, and personal patterns. It does not sell wellness information or create public user profiles.

## Backups

Users may download a standard JSON backup or a password-protected backup. Backup files are created on the device and are not uploaded by Aequimens. Users are responsible for where they store or share those files. Forgotten backup passwords cannot be recovered.

## Notifications

Notification permission is optional. Reminder times are stored locally. Browser and operating-system restrictions may affect whether reminders appear while the app is closed.

## Deletion

Users can reset progress or delete all Aequimens data from Settings. These actions remove only Aequimens-owned browser storage keys and do not clear unrelated browser data.
